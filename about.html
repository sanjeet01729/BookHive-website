About BookHive

BookHive is a premium mobile e-reader designed for lovers of classic literature. Our mission is to make reading the world’s greatest public domain books a beautiful, custom, and gamified experience.

Our Vision

We believe that reading classic books shouldn't feel outdated. BookHive takes classic literature—from *Moby Dick* to *Pride and Prejudice*—and presents them inside a state-of-the-art reading layout featuring drop-caps, left-bordered quotes, and smooth scrolling optimized specifically for mobile screens.

Key Features

  • Offline Books Shelf: Download your favorite classics and read them anywhere, even without an internet connection.
  • Antique Hardcover Styles: Read with specialized fonts (including Dyslexic and Georgia Serif) and customizable background colors (Sepia, Warm Light, Dark, AMOLED).
  • Gamified Reading Milestones: Unlock 10 unique rewards and tracking badges as you build reading habits, complete books, and maintain streaks.
  • Visual Analytics: Beautiful charts showing weekly reading activity, average reading speed, and annual calendars.
